The Majesty of Yosemite

Yosemite National Park is best known for its waterfalls, but within its nearly 1,200 square miles, you can find deep valleys, grand meadows, and ancient giant sequoias.

Whether you are a hiker or a photographer, the granite cliffs of El Capitan offer an unforgettable experience.

Did You Know?

Yosemite was the first territories set aside by the U.S. government for preservation and public use in 1864.
